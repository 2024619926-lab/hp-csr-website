About HP Sustainable Impact

Our Vision

At HP, we believe that one company can change the world. Technology should be a force for good, unlocking opportunities and improving lives globally. We hold ourselves responsible for our ecological and ethical footprint.


Through strategic planning and targeted local activities, we convert innovation into action, helping create long lasting benefits for underrepresented demographics worldwide.

Forest Ecosystem Restoration

Protecting the planet requires hands on dedication. As part of our commitment to forest positive printing, HP employees participate actively in reforestation projects worldwide.


By volunteering to restore damaged ecosystems, clean local woodlands, and plant native species, we help ensure biodiversity thrives for future generations while offsetting atmospheric carbon footprint.
